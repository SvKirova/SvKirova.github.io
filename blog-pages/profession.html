Future dream profession




My dream companies

As you may already know, there is no such thing as the IT professional job. If you are an undergraduate IT professional, you have many options to choose from in the IT field. Almost every organization has to deal with some form of IT. That is why the job market for an IT-professional is huge. You can work in many different areas after graduating. For mid-term decisions it is important to get a more detailed understanding of the IT field and possibilities. Learn more about my dream companies, their company culture and type of work.

I do have some dream companies in mind I would like to work for in the future, such as Apple, Google and Microsoft. The importance and magnitude of these corporations, along with their market share in different parts of our technology-driven world, means that they strive for the best and put all their time and resources towards innovating. I am looking for exactly that – the chance and ability to work with very talented people and help innovate the technology space.

Microsoft

Microsoft is a major global technology company known for its diverse projects, including software development (Windows, Office), cloud computing (Azure), AI and machine learning, and hardware products (Surface, Xbox). They employ a wide range of IT technologies such as Azure cloud services, Power BI for data analytics, and various development frameworks. With over 220,000 employees worldwide, Microsoft operates as a multinational corporation with a strong focus on innovation, inclusivity, and employee growth. Their international presence supports a global customer base, and job roles include software engineers, product managers, data scientists, and cloud specialists, reflecting their broad technological scope and commitment to a collaborative and dynamic work environment.

I want to work at Microsoft because I see it as the perfect place for me to grow and continuously learn. The company’s focus on a growth mindset really aligns with my personal goals—I want to constantly challenge myself, expand my skills, and contribute to projects that make a real difference. Microsoft gives me the opportunity to do just that, whether it’s through working with cutting-edge technologies or collaborating with some of the brightest minds in the industry.

Sources: Microsoft Careers, Microsoft's About Page

Apple

Apple is a leading global technology company recognized for its innovative products, including iPhones, iPads, Macs, and software like iOS and macOS. They employ advanced technologies such as custom-designed silicon chips and machine learning tools. With a workforce of over 150,000 people worldwide, Apple operates as a multinational corporation with a strong focus on design, user experience, and environmental sustainability. Their international presence supports a diverse customer base, and their job roles range from hardware and software engineers to product designers and retail specialists. This wide array of positions reflects Apple’s commitment to creativity, high-quality craftsmanship, and a collaborative work environment.

I would want to work at Apple because of its focus on creating world-class, user-friendly products that shape how people interact with technology. The culture at Apple emphasizes problem-solving, innovation, and pushing boundaries, which aligns with my desire to work in a place where I can contribute to transformative projects. Moreover, Apple’s commitment to privacy and sustainability makes it a company I admire.

Sources: Apple Careers, Apple's About Page

Google

Google is a global tech giant working on diverse projects like search engine technology, cloud computing, AI, and consumer electronics. They use cutting-edge IT technologies such as Kubernetes, TensorFlow, and custom data center hardware. With over 200,000 employees worldwide, Google is a multinational corporation with a strong emphasis on innovation, collaboration, and employee well-being. They operate internationally, providing services and products globally. Job roles at Google range from software engineers and product managers to data scientists and UX/UI designers, reflecting their broad range of operations and commitment to fostering a creative and supportive work environment.

I’d want to work at Google because it represents a unique blend of innovation and opportunity. The chance to be at the forefront of technological advancements and contribute to impactful projects is incredibly motivating.

Sources: Google Careers, Google's About Page
